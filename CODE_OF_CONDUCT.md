# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

As contributors and maintainers of Terrible Cards, we pledge to respect all people.

## Our Standards

We expect community members to be kind, respectful, and collaborative. Harassment and abusive behavior will not be tolerated.

## Reporting

If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior, please contact the maintainers privately.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported and will be reviewed and acted upon by the maintainers.

For full details, see https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/0/code_of_conduct/.
